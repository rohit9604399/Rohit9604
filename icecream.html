The Creamiest No-Churn Strawberry Ice Cream

A refreshing summer treat that's easy to make and utterly delicious!

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 0 minutes
  • Total Time: 10 minutes
  • Servings: 6-8
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hulled and sliced strawberries

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the whole milk, sugar, and vanilla extract.
  3. Fold the milk mixture into the whipped cream until well combined.
  4. Fold in the sliced strawberries.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
