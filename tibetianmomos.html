Tibetan Momos

Discover the delightful taste of Tibetan Momos, a traditional dish enjoyed as a snack or appetizer. These steamed dumplings are filled with a savory meat mixture and are perfect for any occasion.

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes
  • Servings: 4-6
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1/2 pound ground meat (beef, chicken, or pork)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Gradually add water and knead into a smooth dough. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic until golden brown. Add the ground meat and cook until browned. Stir in cabbage, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 5 minutes and let cool.
  3. Roll out the dough into thin sheets and cut into circles. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle. Fold the edges to seal the dumplings.
  4. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 10-15 minutes, or until the dough is cooked through.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with chopped cilantro and a side of dipping sauce, such as a spicy chili sauce or yogurt dip.
  6. Enjoy these authentic Tibetan Momos with friends and family!
